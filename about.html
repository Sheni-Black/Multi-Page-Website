About Afcon

The Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) is Africa's premier international football tournament, organized by the Confederation of African Football (CAF), where national teams from across the continent compete for the title of African champions. First held in 1957, the tournament has grown from just three teams into a major competition featuring 24 nations, known for its intense rivalry, passionate fans, and unpredictable matches. AFCON has produced many iconic moments and legendary players, and it plays a significant role in showcasing African football talent while strengthening unity and pride among participating countries.
